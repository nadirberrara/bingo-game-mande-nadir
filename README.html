Rules of the Amazing BINGO: As a rule, the game starts when all the players buy bingo tickets. A bingo ticket is a card with a 5x5 grid. 5 columns on the card correspond to 5 letters of the name horizontally of the game "B-I-N-G-O". 24 numbers per each card are random from the limits of 1 to 75. The center of the card is an empty space Each player is provide a unique scorecard so you must be attentive.
